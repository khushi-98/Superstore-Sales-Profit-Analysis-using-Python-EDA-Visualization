# Superstore-Sales-Profit-Analysis-using-Python-EDA-Visualization
---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs an in-depth analysis of a retail e-commerce dataset (Superstore) using Python. The goal is to uncover business insights related to sales, profit, customer segments, and product categories through data cleaning, transformation, and visualization.

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional sales data with the following key features:

* Order Date & Ship Date
* Sales & Profit
* Product Category & Sub-Category
* Customer Segment
* Region & Location

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
* Plotly (Interactive Visualizations)
* Jupyter Notebook / Google Colab

---

## 🔍 Data Preprocessing

* Converted **Order Date** and **Ship Date** into datetime format
* Extracted:

  * Order Month
  * Order Year
  * Day of the Week
* Checked for data types, null values, and dataset structure

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 Sales Analysis

* Monthly sales trend
* Sales distribution by category
* Sales by sub-category

---

### 💰 Profit Analysis

* Monthly profit trends
* Profit distribution by category
* Profit by sub-category

---

### 👥 Customer Segment Analysis

* Sales vs Profit comparison across segments
* Identification of high-performing segments

---

### 📉 Sales-to-Profit Ratio

* Calculated efficiency of each customer segment
* Helped identify segments with high revenue but low profitability

---

## 📊 Visualizations

* Line Charts → Monthly Sales & Profit Trends
* Pie Charts → Category-wise Sales & Profit Distribution
* Bar Charts → Sub-category Analysis
* Comparative Charts → Sales vs Profit by Segment

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Certain categories generate high sales but lower profits
* Seasonal trends are visible in monthly sales and profit
* Customer segments differ significantly in profitability
* Some sub-categories contribute disproportionately to revenue
